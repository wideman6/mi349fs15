E-Sports


League of Legends

The League of Legends World Championship occurred recently. Competing against 15 other teams, SK Telecom T1 was able to take home the trophy along with $1,000,000 in prize money.They did so in stunning fashion, only losing a single game in the 18 games they played.

Hearthstone

The Hearthstone World Championship this year was taken home by Ostaka. Ostaka is a Hearthstone player from Sweden currently playing for Natus Vincere and he was able to take home $100,000 for his victory. This is only the second World Championship for Hearthstone, but the game has grown a massive fan base to support it.

Starcraft II

Kim Yoo Jin, otherwise known as sOs has taken home his second World Championship in a row for $100,000. sOs has won that last three tournaments where the winner has gotten $100,000 or more, and has thus earned the nickname $O$ from a lot of people. It is remarkable what he is able to accomplish on a large stage.
