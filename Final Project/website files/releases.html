New Releases


Fallout 4

Fallout 4 is possibly the most hyped game to ever be released. Made by Bethesda Studios, this is the 7th game in the Fallout series. Players venture through a large world similar to skyrim, and complete various missions. The game also holds a huge amount of customizability options when it comes to your character and their guns.

Star Wars: Battlefront

The new Battlefront game is another game people have long awaited. The game positions players on a large battlefield with many other players, and you try to complete objectives. The game also comes out right around the same time as the new Star Wars movie, making everybody extremely excited for everything new in the Star Wars Universe.
