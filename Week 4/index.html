One Pager Website

Short Pitch

A website dedicated to providing up to date information on the gaming industry.

Long Pitch

This website will focus on providing consistent, up to date informations on every aspect of the gaming industry. The website will provide detailed discussion on games currently in development, reviews of many popular and new video games, and reporting on professional gaming events. The goal of the website is to provide a go-to place for gamers to get news regarding their favorite hobby, as well as to inspire discussion across readers and promote new and upcoming games.
